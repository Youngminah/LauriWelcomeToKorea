## Seoul (Capital City)

### 1. Gyeongbokgung Palace
<img width="800" height="534" alt="CAN_459511" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4d59c38b-ec3f-4a23-9bd0-88798ee20bd3" />

- Largest royal palace, built 1395.
- Closed Tuesdays
- You can wear a hanbok (rent nearby) — cute photos!
- Lauri If you don't like sightseeing(LandMark), you can skip it.
- Bonus Seoul spots
   - Bukchon Hanok Village — traditional houses, 5-min walk from the palace.
   - Gwangjang Market — street food (mung-bean pancakes, mayak gimbap).
   - N Seoul Tower — sunset city views from Namsan mountain.

### 2. Lotte World
<img width="520" height="371" alt="AA 29377827 1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5b195fd3-b9d3-4c9d-bae7-0292c63604d6" />

- Korea's Disneyland — same magical-castle vibe.
- Located in Jamsil, Seoul (subway line 2, Jamsil Station).
- Half indoor / half outdoor — perfect for any weather.
- Stay until evening for the lit-up castle & parade.
- Recently, there has been a MapleStory-themed(really Popular Game In Korea) event.

### 1. Seongsan Ilchulbong
<img width="550" height="324" alt="103351_112444_5538" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4674cc4f-9719-439f-89e9-f7cce4d2a424" />

- 5,000-year-old volcanic tuff cone rising from the sea.
- 25-min climb up wooden stairs to the crater rim.
- Famous for sunrise — arrive in the dark!

### 2. Hamdeok Beach

<img width="1200" height="800" alt="Jeju-Hamdeok-Seoubong-Beach3" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/16619c23-3314-4c45-9e0f-c8da1f9a728c" />

- Emerald sea !!
- Turquoise water, white sand — calm and shallow.
- Best in April — surrounded by canola flower fields.
- 30 min by car from Jeju Airport.
